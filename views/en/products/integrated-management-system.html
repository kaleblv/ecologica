Compliance

Safety Compliance

Whether it is common management standards such as OHSAS 18001, or compliance with regulatory requirements such as OSHA, WCB, RIDDOR and others, companies are frequently required to keep extensive and detailed accounts of their environmental, health and safety information.

OSHA, WCB and RIDDOR Compliance

Intelex’s Business Management software system streamlines the collection, tracking and reporting of your workplace health and safety information and accident, incident and injury reporting. All of the data is input into a centralized database and can be merged into the reporting forms required by different regulatory bodies. If you report to regulatory bodies other than OSHA, WCB and RIDDOR, specialized merge templates are easily created to accomodate your organization’s specific needs.

OHSAS 18001

Many organizations worldwide adhere to the management practices outlined in the OHSAS 18001 standard. Following these standards helps reduce employee risk, improve the effectiveness of Occupational Health and Safety management, and demonstrate diligence in cultivating a strong safety culture.

The components of the Intelex Business Management software system speak directly to requirements within the OHSAS 18001. Specifically:

  • General Occupational Health and Safety Management
  • Safety Policy
  • Planning
  • Safety Objectives
  • Safety Audit Management
  • Communication Management
  • Training Management
  • Documentation Storage and Revisioning
  • Injury and Incident Reporting
  • Monitoring and Measurement
Quality Compliance

The ISO 9001 standard sets out the criteria for a quality management system and is based on a number of quality management principles including a strong customer focus, the motivation and implication of top management, the process approach and continual improvement. It can be used by any organization, large or small, regardless of its field of activity and is implemented by over one million companies and organizations in over 170 countries.

The Intelex Business Management software system has been specifically designed to allign with each component of the ISO 9001 standard and to facilitate and maintain seamless compliance with it. Specifically:

  • Section 5.3 – Quality Policy
  • Section 5.4 – Planning
  • Section 4.0 – Quality Management System
  • Section 5.4.1 -Quality Objectives
  • Section 5.5 – Responsibility, Authority and Communication
  • Section 6.2.2 – Compentancy, Awareness and Training
  • Section 7.2.3 – Customer Communications
  • Section 4.2 – Document Requirements
  • Section 4.2.3 – Document Control
  • Section 8.0 – Measurement, Analysis and Improvement
  • Section 8.5.2 – Corrective Action
  • Section 8.5.3 – Preventive Action
  • Section 8.2 – Monitoring and Measurement
  • Section 8.3 – Control of Nonconformity
  • Section 8.4 – Analysis of Data
  • Section 8.2.2 – Internal Audit
  • Section 5.6 – Management Review
